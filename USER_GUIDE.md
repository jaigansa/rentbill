# User Guide - Rent Bill Pro 📖

Welcome to the official user guide. This document explains how to navigate and use the features of Rent Bill Pro effectively.

## 1. Getting Started
- **Default PIN**: When you first launch the app, the default login PIN is `1234`.
- **Change PIN**: Go to **Settings > Credentials** to set your own secure 4-digit Master PIN.

## 2. Setting Up Receiving Accounts
Before registering tenants, add the accounts where you receive money:
1. Go to **Settings > Receiving Accounts**.
2. Enter the **Owner Name** (e.g., John Doe).
3. Select **Account Type** (UPI or Bank).
4. Fill in the details and click **Add Account Record**.
*These accounts will be used to assign owners to units and populate "Received By" dropdowns.*

## 3. Managing Tenants
- **Registration**: Use **Settings > Register Unit** to add a new tenant. Assign an owner/account to each unit.
- **Editing**: Click on any tenant card in the directory to update their details or move-in info.
- **Vacating**: Use the "Mark Vacant" option to initiate a final settlement.
- **Archiving**: Deleting a tenant performs a "Soft Delete," keeping their payment history for your financial reports.

## 4. Monthly Billing
1. Go to the **Billing** section.
2. Find the unit and enter the **Current EB Reading**.
3. Click the **Plus Icon** to generate the bill.
- **Arrears**: If a tenant has debt from last month, it will automatically show as a red badge and be added to the new bill.

## 5. Recording Payments
1. Go to **History & Records**.
2. Select a unit.
3. Click the **Checkmark Icon** on a pending bill.
4. Select who **Received** the money and the **Amount Paid**.
- **Partial Payments**: If the tenant pays less than the total, select an adjustment:
    - **Carry Forward**: Balance goes to next month's bill.
    - **Discount**: Forgive the balance as a waiver.
    - **Write-Off**: Record the balance as a business loss.

## 6. Financial Tracking (Dashboard)
- **Account Settlements**: View exactly how much income each owner has generated and what their current balance is.
- **Owner Timeline**: Click **Timeline** on an owner card to see every rupee added or withdrawn by that person.
- **Cash in Hand**: The **Total Balance** card shows: `(Paid Rent + Advances) - (Expenses + Payouts)`.

## 7. Audit & Reports
- Select a month in **Settings > Audit & Reports**.
- Click **Generate Audit** to see a full financial breakdown including a printable transaction log.

## 8. Data Safety (Backup & Restore)
- **Backup**: Regularly click **Download Backup** in Settings to save your data to your device.
- **Restore**: If you move to a new device, select your `.db` file, enter your PIN, and click **Upload & Restore**.
